# Code Of Conduct

## Our standard

We want RelayDesk to be a respectful, welcoming project for contributors of different backgrounds and experience levels.

Examples of expected behavior:

- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume good intent, but give clear feedback
- Focus on ideas, code, and product outcomes rather than personal attacks
- Help newcomers understand context instead of gatekeeping

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation
- Personal insults or hostile review behavior
- Publishing private information without permission
- Repeated disruptive conduct after feedback

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict access when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to the maintainers before escalating publicly.
